RaenyTuesday

A 2 year photography project on Instagram, I documented my daily Adventures with a photo a day. Please feel free to the browse the feed on my Instagram account, @mnefertiti, below is the accumulation of 200 days of exploring the world through image storytelling.

Adventures 2015 & 2016
2015

The project began in the Summer of 2015 – when I decided I wanted to start living my best life, from the inside. Using what I had at my disposal, I set out on an Adventure to, every day, try something new, explore a new place, meet new people - documenting each life changing experience in a photograph on my Instagram, @mnefertiti, with the caption “#adventures #dayX”. And, because of that fateful day and this project, I live every day as an Adventure.

2016

My second year of Adventures evolved, this time focusing on the short form photography medium. Each experienced was summed up in a quote, each photograph aesthetically developed. For the second year in a row, the project-that-changed-my-life gave these 100 days purpose, and gave me the impetus to live my best life. 100 days. I urge you to try to create or do something for 100 days, and see how you grow and explore and adventure.
